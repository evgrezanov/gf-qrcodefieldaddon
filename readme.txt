=== QR-code reader field For Gravity Forms  ===
Tags: QR Reader, Gravity Forms, Gravity Forms QR Reader
Tested up to: 5.4
Requires PHP: 5.0

== Description ==
**Gravity Forms QR Reader Field** is a easy to use setup QR reader field in your Gravity Form for your [WordPress](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WordPress) site.
Plugin based [html5-qrcode](https://github.com/mebjas/html5-qrcode) library.
**[Demo](http://github.com/evgrezanov/)**

> **Plugin Features**
>
> * Multiple Tooltip Position
> * Step Adding Option
> * Min and Max Option in slider
> * Prefix On Digit
> * Tooltip color option
> * Compatible in all major browser
> * Easy use
